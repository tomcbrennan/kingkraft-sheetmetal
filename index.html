ABOUT US

Your local sheetmetal ductwork & steel fabrication specialists.

Kingkraft Sheetmetal specialises in the manufacture of sheetmetal ductwork to suit all requirements, from small residential projects to large commercial buildings.

The business was established in 2004 as a private family business, and has been operating from Bayswater WA since inception. We focus on customer satisfaction by providing excellent service, as well as delivering high quality products.

Kingkraft Sheetmetal can also assist with any general sheetmetal and steel fabrication. Give us a call to get professional advice and get a quote on your project.
